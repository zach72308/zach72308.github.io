SEMI-AQUATIC CRUSTACEANS

Mr&Mrs Crabs

Mr and Mrs Crabs are red claw crabs, whick originate from mangrove swamps in Asia. They live in slightly brackish water and are omnivores. They are relativly small crabs, fitting easily in the palm of my hand.
These crabs are very easy to care for, but can leave some food to rot sometimes, so they have take a nominal amount of my sanity.

SEMI-TERRESTRIAL CRUSTACEANS

Hermit Crabs

Terrestrial hermit crabs are social animals that need at least on cohabitant of the same species. They are scavenging prey animals that need deep substrate to burrow in and plenty of food thats rich in protein and calcium to thrive. These crabs switch shells every so often to allow themselves a cofortable place to grow in, and will go into their burrows for long periods of time to molt(similar to shedding skin).
Hermit Crabs are relativly low maintenance and can be very friendly to their humans, and they take none of my sanity to take care of.
